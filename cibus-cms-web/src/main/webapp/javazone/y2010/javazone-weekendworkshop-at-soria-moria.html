It's a cold, white, winter day in Norway. On a Saturday morning at 09.00am, while the rest of the population is out skiing, we find the JZ Crew creating their own dream - JavaZone 2010! The place is Soria Moria Conference Center and we can feel the tension. The atmosphere is strangely calm, but there seem to be going on a lot in the parallel sessions.

"We try to work as much as possible when we meet - and talk less, says Andreas" He's just found a good name for a new concept this year: JZ JourneyZone.
