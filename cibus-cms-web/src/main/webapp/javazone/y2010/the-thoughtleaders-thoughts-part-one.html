The Thought leaders thoughts - part one

As always JavaZone search ways to improve and renew the conference from year to year. In November we gathered 30 of the thought leaders, chief scientists, CTOs from our partners and leading IT-companies in Norway in a workshop to help us with the question: "What are the hot Java related topics for 2010?"

Some of the results from the workshop told us:

  • "We want more experience reports - hopefully WITHOUT the 'Hollywood Happy Endings'"
  • "It's about time customers speak up and let us understand where they come from and what they experience"
  • "I'd like to develop leading edge knowledge on developing alternative languages on the JVM"

Hopefully this will give some indicators on what we value in your abstracts this year!
